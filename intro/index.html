Aviation Competition Summary

Aviation comes in many flaws that need to be addressed. Two major airline companies competing are Airbus and Boeing, you’ve probably been on one. Boeing is a company that specialized in commercial jetliners from the Boeing-707 to Boeing-787 family. Airbus is a company that specialized in commercial jetliners from the Airbus-A300 to Airbus-A380 family. What is the problem that needs to be addressed? These companies rush to release newer models to compete on the market. Both companies are known for making fuel-efficient, sound efficient models that airlines buy often. This means that if one company can offer a better plane the other can lose a lot of money. This is a real world problem as “rushed aircraft” tend to lead to more catastrophic circumstances that can cost other’s lives. Take the Boeing 737-Max as an example.

  • Airbus is the world's largest airliner manufacturer and took the most airliner orders, surpassing rival Boeing. It designs, manufactures and sells civil and military aerospace products worldwide and manufactures aircraft in the European Union and various other countries.
  • Boeing is the world's largest aerospace company and leading manufacturer of commercial jetliners, defense, space and security systems, and service provider of aftermarket support.
